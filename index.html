How does it work?

You send the source materials from which your video content will be generated. Each element: voice file, background music file, subtitle lines, images, as well as built-in fonts and color palette, is used in the assembly of each video in random order. The core of the video is the voiceover advertising file. Each element is an independent unit of information not logically tied to another. This video-content structure allows you to create an unlimited number of video combinations, and well-crafted voice tracks, short and concise subtitles, and vivid, informative photos/images—together with a wide range of transitions, fonts, and colors—help produce content with high retention among a “cold” audience!
